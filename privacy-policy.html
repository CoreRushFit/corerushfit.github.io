Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 30/07/2025

Introduction

Welcome to CoreRushFit. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our platform, available at corerush.fit, and use our services to discover and interact with fitness studios.

Information We Collect

  1. Personal Information
    • Name
    • Email address
    • Date of birth
    • Profile picture (if provided)
  2. Usage Information
    • Pages visited and features used
    • Bookings, reviews, and preferences
  3. Transaction Information
    • Payment methods (processed via secure third-party payment processors)
    • History of bookings or purchases
  4. Location Information

    If you grant permission, we may collect your device’s location to show nearby fitness studios.

  5. Information from Fitness Studios

    When you book or interact with a studio, we may receive confirmation or feedback from the studio.

How We Use Your Information

  • Providing and managing our services
  • Creating and maintaining your account
  • Facilitating bookings and interactions with fitness studios
  • Processing payments and sending confirmations
  • Sending administrative communications and updates
  • Personalizing your experience and recommendations
  • Aggregating data for analytics and service improvements
  • Complying with legal obligations

How We Share Your Information

  • With Fitness Studios: Your information may be shared with studios you interact or book with (e.g., name, booking details).
  • Service Providers: Third parties assisting us with payment processing, analytics, email delivery, and technical support.
  • Legal and Safety: When required to comply with laws, regulations, or legal processes, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of users or others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, monitor usage, and collect information about your activity on our platform. You may adjust your browser settings to manage cookies, but some features may not function properly as a result.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to also take steps to protect your information.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Request a copy of your data in a commonly used format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@corerush.fit

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
